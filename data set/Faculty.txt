## Overview

HACA mentors are active industry professionals working across:

* Digital Marketing

* Motion Graphics
* Full Stack Development
* Artificial Intelligence

The institution follows a practical mentor-led ecosystem where students learn directly from professionals handling real-world projects and clients.
