# HACA Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is HACA?

HACA (Haris & Co Academy) is an industry-focused learning ecosystem offering practical education in Marketing, Design, and Technology through real projects, mentorship, and internships.

### Where is HACA located?

HACA operates campuses in:

* Kozhikode, Kerala, India
* Dubai, UAE

### Are courses available online?

Yes. Most courses are available in:

* Offline mode
* Online live mode
* Hybrid mode

### Do I need prior experience?

No. Most programs are beginner-friendly and start from fundamentals.

### What is the teaching style?

HACA follows:

* 90% practical learning
* 10% theory
* Real-world project-based training

### Is there placement support?

Yes. HACA provides:

* Resume building
* Mock interviews
* Portfolio guidance
* Career mentoring
* Placement assistance

### Are internships included?

Yes. Long-term flagship programs include internship opportunities with real projects.

### Is EMI available?

Yes. EMI is available for eligible long-duration courses.

### Can college students join?

Yes. College students can join online or weekend batches.

### Can working professionals join?

Yes. Evening and weekend online batches are designed for working professionals.
