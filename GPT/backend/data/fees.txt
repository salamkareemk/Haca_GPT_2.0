# HACA Fee Structure Dataset

## Overview
HACA provides flexible payment structures for all schools under its ecosystem, including Marketing School, Design School, and Tech School. Fee payments can be completed through full payment, EMI, or installment-based methods.

# Payment Policies

## Accepted Payment Methods
- UPI
- Bank Transfer
- Debit/Credit Cards
- EMI Financing
- International Transfer (Dubai Campus)

---

## EMI Policy
- EMI available for courses above ₹35,000
- Minimum down payment: 20%
- EMI duration: 3–12 months
- Partner financing options available

---

## Refund Policy
| Situation | Refund Eligibility |
|---|---|
| Before course start | 80% refundable |
| Within first 7 days | 50% refundable |
| After 7 days | Non-refundable |
