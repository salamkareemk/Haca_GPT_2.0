# Placement FAQs

### Is placement guaranteed?

HACA provides strong placement support and hiring assistance, but final selection depends on student performance.

### Are internships paid?

Some internships may be paid depending on the company and role.

### Can students freelance after course completion?

Yes. Students are encouraged to build freelance profiles and personal brands.

### Is remote placement possible?

Yes. Remote job opportunities may be available.

### Do students receive certificates?

Yes. Students receive course completion certificates and project experience documentation.
