# HACA Policies & Guidelines Dataset

## Overview

This document contains the institutional policies, student guidelines, payment rules, academic policies, and operational standards followed across:

* Marketing School
* Design School
* Tech School

The policies are structured for:

* Student awareness
* Administrative clarity
* RAG chatbot retrieval
* Future automation systems

---

# Admission Policy

## Eligibility Criteria

### General Eligibility

* Open to students from all educational backgrounds
* No prior experience required for beginner-level programs
* Working professionals may also apply
* International students can join online programs

---

## Admission Process

### Step-by-Step Admission Flow

1. Submit enquiry form
2. Attend counselling session
3. Choose course & batch
4. Complete registration
5. Confirm payment
6. Receive onboarding details

---

## Required Documents

Students may need:

* Government ID proof
* Passport-size photograph
* Educational qualification details
* Contact information

---

## Seat Allocation Policy

* Admissions are processed on first-come-first-served basis
* Limited seats available in cohort-based batches
* Seat confirmation only after registration payment

---

## Attendance Policy

### Minimum Attendance Requirement

# Refund Policy

## Refund Conditions

| Situation           | Refund Status            |
| ------------------- | ------------------------ |
| Before course start | Partial refund possible  |
| Within first 7 days | Limited refund possible  |
| After 7 days        | Generally non-refundable |

---

## Refund Processing Time

Refund processing may take:

* 7–21 working days

---

## Non-Refundable Situations

Refund may not apply if:

* Student voluntarily discontinues
* Course access already provided
* Recorded materials downloaded
